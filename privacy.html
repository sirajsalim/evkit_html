Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

Information We Collect

EVKit is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Vehicle data you enter (stored locally on your device)
  • App usage analytics (anonymous)
  • Crash reports (if you opt in)

How We Use Your Information

Your vehicle data is stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud (if enabled). We never sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage

All personal data is stored on your device and in your private iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

We use Apple's CloudKit for iCloud sync. Your data is encrypted and protected by your Apple ID.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Email us at privacy@evkit.app
